Embossed interior cladding panels. A wall surface worked in deep relief — light, shadow and a calmer room, pressed into the material itself. The newest member of the Beyond Rational range; final formats and photography are on their way.

The idea

A surface that
does more than cover.

01
Relief, not print

The pattern is embossed into the panel — real depth that catches light and throws shadow as you move past it. Nothing applied on top to wear away.

02
Cladding that calms

Designed by an acoustics studio. The worked surface breaks up reflections, taking the hard edge off a room while it dresses the wall.

03
Made to be lived with

Conscious materials, built for interiors that should still look considered in ten years. Specification and end-of-life detail to follow.
