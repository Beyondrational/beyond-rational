Wuud.

Embossed wooden cladding for interior and exterior architecture. Real wood, modified to hold deep relief, then finished natural, translucent or painted. Developed in Finland; brought to Denmark by Beyond Rational.

The idea

Wood, worked
into relief.

01
Embossed, not printed

A novel wood-modifying process presses pattern into the surface of real timber — true depth that catches light and throws shadow. Standard patterns or fully custom.

02
Inside and out

The same modification that forms the relief weatherproofs the wood and adds fire retardancy — so one panel language runs from interior walls to building façades.

03
From Finnish research

Developed out of a Business Finland & Aalto University research programme. Conscious, contemporary cladding — now available through Beyond Rational in Denmark.
