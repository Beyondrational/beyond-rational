Material study

Wood
fibre,
foam-formed.

The tile is built by suspending wood fibre in foam, not by pressing it. The result is an open, porous structure that captures sound across a wide frequency range, while the surface stays calm and tactile.

It belongs on the same drawing as the limestone, the oak, the brass.
