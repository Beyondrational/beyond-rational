Sappa.

Acoustic panels grown from hemp — a fast-renewing natural fibre, bound with a cement-free mineral binder. For walls and ceilings, brought to Denmark by Beyond Rational.

The idea

Grown, not
manufactured.

01
A fast-renewing raw material

Hemp needs little water, no pesticides, and reaches full height in months, not years — capturing atmospheric carbon roughly twice as efficiently as forestry across its growing cycle.

02
Zero-waste by construction

The panels are pressed from stem chips — the residue left over once fibre is stripped from the plant for other industrial uses. Material that would otherwise be discarded becomes the product.

03
Cradle to cradle

A cement-free mineral binder holds the hemp chips together, with no synthetic flame retardants added — the panel is naturally fire-resistant and recyclable at end of life.
