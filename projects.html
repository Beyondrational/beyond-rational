The cases

What happened in the room

Ångtvätten 22

Stora Essingen, Stockholm · Commercial office, 40,000 m² property

Aisti® tiles in dark blue, 600×600 mm at 20 mm, installed in the standard T-grid by a professional team. The tiles absorb sound reflections and reduce echo — installed side-by-side with mineral wool to show no difference in acoustic performance.

Bulevardi 2

Helsinki city centre · Commercial office

Aisti® tiles in white, 600×1200 mm at 40 mm, ceiling-mounted. Reverberation time — the time for sound to decay 60 dB — improved roughly 29% across the octave bands after installation.
