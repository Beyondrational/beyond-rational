What we supply

We are the official distributor of Aisti® in Denmark. The Aisti® Acoustic Tile is a bio-based absorber made from natural wood fibre — Absorption Class A, no synthetic binders, no plastics, no irritating fibres.

See the tile See cases

The technology

Foam-formed,
not pressed.

Aisti's foam-forming technology, adapted from the Finnish paper industry, suspends wood fibre in foam instead of pressing it flat. The fibres set into an open, porous structure that captures sound across a wide frequency range — without binders or plastics.

The technology is cost-effective and energy-conscious, producing tiles that are light in weight, plastic-free, and 100% recyclable. Produced in Jyväskylä, Finland.
