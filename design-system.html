A surface
that disappears
into the room.

The visual system is one family at five weights, a warm cream body, and a single charred-oak band that does the brand work. Nothing is layered on top of a photograph; the page itself carries the weight.

This file is the canonical reference. Type, surface, spec table, and material panel ship from here to the website, the slide template, and the product datasheet.

Motion

Restrained,
by intent.

Magnetic primary.

The button pulls toward the cursor inside a 120 px radius, then snaps back on leave. Used once per page for the dominant action; never decorative.

Slide-fill secondary.

Ink rises from the bottom in 520 ms ease-out-expo; text inverts to paper. One transform, two read positions. Reserved for secondary CTAs that need weight without competing with the primary.

Character reveal.

Display titles split per character on scroll-intersect. Each char rises 110 % with a 24 ms stagger. Words stay together; only the line wrapper masks.

Hung in space.

Number counter.

Stats animate from zero to target on intersect over 1.4 s with ease-out-quart and tabular numerals. Used on the spec sheet, never on decorative numbers.

R · Columns (v1.4)

Image + text, four ways.

One component (.bra-columns), three modifiers. Heading size is capped per-column (not tied to full viewport width), so a long unbroken word can never overflow past the media — the bug that showed up on the Aisti technology block.

01 · Media left, text centered

The technology

Foam-formed, not pressed.

Suspending wood fibre in foam instead of pressing it flat leaves an open, porous structure that captures sound across a wide frequency range.

02 · Media right

The technology

Foam-formed, not pressed.

Same pair, mirrored — use when the previous section already ended on an image, to keep the page from repeating its own rhythm.

03 · Stacked, media on top

The technology

Foam-formed, not pressed.

A wide, cinematic image leads, then a narrower reading column follows below a rule — good when the photograph needs the full width to read.

04 · Ruled, equal split

The technology

Foam-formed, not pressed.

An exact 50/50 split, top-aligned, with a vertical rule as the seam — the most formal, catalogue-plate version of the pair.
